# Facility Usage Inputs and Yields

## Monster Feed
- Using Monster Feed always requires **1 Apple** per use.
- Each use yields **more pigs and chickens** (yield increases with upgrades), but always only **one monster per feed**.
- Yield is not just durability; upgrading increases the number of animals produced.

## Field Planting
- Planting a farm (Field/Plantation) always requires **1 Nuts** per planting.
- Each harvest from a Field yields **1 farm produce** (e.g., wheat, vegetables, etc.).

## Field Harvest
- Each Field produces **1 farm produce** per harvest cycle.

---

These mechanics are confirmed in-game and supplement the main facility research notes.
